About me

I have always been interested in psychology and how the mind works. I studied cognitive science 2019 at the University of Gothenburg. There I read a course in interaction design where we analyzed how people interact with technology. This helped me understand why it is important to improve usability and how we can do it. After that course, I decided to study UX / UI design fully and it was one of the best decisions I have made.

What I like most about UX / UI is the collaboration process that involves teamwork but at the same time be able to work independently. Being able to work with creative people and share ideas is the reason why I love UX / UI and feel that I have really found my calling. In my free time I enjoy watching thriller movies, going out with my friends or reading a book by my favorite author Agatha Christie.
